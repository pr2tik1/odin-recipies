Reshami Paneer

What is Reshami Paneer?

Reshami Paneer is a delectable Indian vegetarian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) that is marinated in a creamy and flavorful gravy. It is known for its silky and smooth texture, making it a delightful addition to Indian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Yogurt
  • Cream
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices (e.g., turmeric, coriander, cumin)
  • Butter or ghee

How to Make Reshami Paneer

  1. Marinate paneer cubes in a mixture of yogurt, cream, and spices.
  2. Saute onions and tomatoes in butter or ghee until soft.
  3. Add the marinated paneer and cook until the gravy thickens.
  4. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.

Enjoy the creamy and silky goodness of Reshami Paneer with naan or rice!
