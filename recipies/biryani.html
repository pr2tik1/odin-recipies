Biryani

What is Biryani?

Biryani is a popular and flavorful South Asian rice dish made with aromatic spices, long-grain basmati rice, and various proteins such as chicken, lamb, or vegetables. It is known for its rich taste and fragrant aroma.

Types of Biryani

  • Chicken Biryani
  • Vegetable Biryani
  • Lamb Biryani
  • Beef Biryani
  • Hyderabadi Biryani

How to Make Chicken Biryani

  1. Wash and marinate chicken pieces with yogurt, spices, and herbs.
  2. Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes.
  3. In a large pot, heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until it changes color.
  5. Add spices and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Layer half of the partially cooked rice over the chicken.
  7. Sprinkle saffron milk and fried onions on top.
  8. Layer the remaining rice and cover the pot with a tight lid.
  9. Cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.
  10. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken biryani!
