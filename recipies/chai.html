Chai (Tea)

What is Chai?

Chai, also known as tea, is a popular and aromatic beverage made by steeping tea leaves or tea bags in hot water. It is a beloved beverage enjoyed by people around the world and is often prepared with various spices and milk for added flavor and richness.

Types of Chai

  • Masala Chai
  • Green Tea
  • Black Tea
  • Herbal Tea
  • Iced Tea

.... and more !

How to Make Masala Chai

  1. Boil water in a pot and add tea leaves or tea bags.
  2. Simmer for a few minutes until the tea is brewed to your desired strength.
  3. Add milk, sugar, and spices (e.g., cardamom, cinnamon, ginger) to the pot.
  4. Simmer the mixture, stirring occasionally, until it comes to a boil.
  5. Strain the chai into cups and serve hot.

Enjoy a warm and comforting cup of masala chai!
